How to Brew

1

Water Temperature

Heat fresh water to 80°C (175°F) for green tea. For black tea, bring water to a full boil (100°C/212°F).

2

Measure Tea

Use 1 teaspoon (2g) of tea leaves per 8oz (240ml) cup. Adjust to taste.

3

Steep Time

Steep for 2-3 minutes. Avoid over-steeping to prevent bitterness.

4

Enjoy

Remove tea leaves and enjoy your perfectly brewed cup of tea.

Ingredients

100% pure tea leaves. No additives or artificial flavors.

Our teas are sourced from sustainable farms and are certified organic.
